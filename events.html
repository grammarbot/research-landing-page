Events

Events we attend

We regularly attend, present, and publish our work at scholarly research conferences, as well as community conferences, hackathons, and events within the Wikimedia Movement. Our most common venues are the following:

  • CSCW: ACM Conference on Computer-Supported Cooperative Work and Social Computing
  • CHI: ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems
  • ICWSM: AAAI International Conference on Web and Social Media
  • WWW: The Web Conference
  • Wikimania: the Wikimedia Foundation annual conference of
  • Wikimedia Hackathon: the Wikimedia Foundation Annual Hackathon

Event series we run

Wikimedia Research also runs the following event series, devoted to furthering and disseminating scholarship around Wikimedia projects and open collaboration.

  • Wikimedia Research Showcase: A monthly public showcase of recent research by Research team members, collaborators, and guest presenters from the scholarly research community.
  • Wiki Workshop: An annual forum hosted at a major scholarly conference bringing together researchers exploring all aspects of Wikipedia and other Wikimedia projects.
  • WikiCite Conference: An annual conference that brings together librarians, modelers, Wikimedia contributors and open data stakeholders to develop a centralized bibliographic database of source and citation metadata in Wikidata to serve Wikimedia projects and, eventually, the sum of all human knowledge
