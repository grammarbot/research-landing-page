Increasing Diversity in Free Knowledge Production

We are conducting experiments to increase the diversity of Wikimedia projects by engaging and retaining contributors from underrepresented groups.

Project overview

The Wikipedia community has long acknowledged the lack of diversity among Wikipedia contributors, most of whom are white males. In fact, only about 10–15% of Wikipedia’s editing pool is female, and the group lacks diversity in other key areas, including race, socioeconomic status, age, and even personality type.

This lack of diversity has led to important flaws and biases in Wikipedia’s encyclopedic content as well as a large untapped pool of potential contributors who have not been given adequate voice on the platform. On top of that, the social environment Wikipedia editors contribute in can be affected by this lack of diversity. Thus, the goal of this project is to increase diversity among editors to develop socio-technical solutions to engage and retain people from underrepresented groups.
